Lessons & Teaching Style

I would like to offer different lessons depends on the needs of students . For beginner, intermediate or students with good level will get lessons in related to their interesting parts of learning either starting with Self Introductions, How to read and count numbers, Personal pronouns and how to use tenses in formulate sentences , Pronunciations, How to read time in swahili and different practical subjects. I usual prefer to use Skype, Google Meet or Zoom but i am flexible depends on what is easy for the students to access. I can also use white Board. Students get opportunities to ask questions and get answers and they get opportunity to practice with me what they have been learning.
